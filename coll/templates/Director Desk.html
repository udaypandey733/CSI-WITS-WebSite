About College

CSI WITS established in year 2015 , is one of the premier engineering colleges in the state of Telangana. WITS is sponsored by CHURCH OF SOUTH INDIA MEDAK DIOCESE ,for the past 100 years. WITS is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad.
